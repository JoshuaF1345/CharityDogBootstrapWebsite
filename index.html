What can Charity do?

She can help anxious students (and staff). She can attend exams, counselling sessions and classes, as well as offer 1:1 support if required

How can Weymouth Keep Charity?

Any fundraising we can do to help fund Charity will cover the cost of her insurance, food, vets bills, check ups etc. We need to evidence that Charity helps the college students.

Where is Charity Based?

Charity is based in student HQ with her human Nola Smith.
